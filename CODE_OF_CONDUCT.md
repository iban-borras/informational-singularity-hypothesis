# 🌱 Code of Conduct — HSI Agents Project

This project is committed to fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and intellectually open environment for all contributors, regardless of background, discipline, or identity.

We believe that respect, curiosity, and collaboration are essential for meaningful scientific exploration — especially in transdisciplinary fields like Informational Singularity.

---

## 💬 Our Values

* **Openness to ideas**: We embrace diverse perspectives and encourage bold hypotheses.
* **Respectful dialogue**: Debate is welcomed, but personal attacks, condescension, or hostility will not be tolerated.
* **Constructive feedback**: Suggestions should be focused on improvement, not criticism.
* **Credit and transparency**: Contributions will be attributed. All voices matter.

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

* Discrimination, harassment, or exclusion based on identity or background
* Aggressive or demeaning language
* Plagiarism or misrepresentation of ideas
* Spam, trolling, or off-topic disruptions

---

## 🧭 Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, temporary exclusion, or removal from the project, depending on severity. Issues can be reported via GitHub or directly to the project maintainer (Iban).

---

## 🤝 Let’s build a space of meaningful emergence

We are exploring how complexity and harmony can arise from nothingness. Let’s ensure our community reflects those same principles.

Thank you for helping create a space of respect, inspiration, and shared wonder.
