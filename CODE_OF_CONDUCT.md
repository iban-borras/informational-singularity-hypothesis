# 🌱 Code of Conduct — ISH Agents Project

This project is committed to fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and intellectually open environment for all contributors, regardless of background, discipline, or identity.

We believe that respect, curiosity, and collaboration are essential for meaningful scientific exploration — especially in transdisciplinary fields like the Informational Singularity Hypothesis (ISH).

---

## 💬 Our Values

* **Openness to ideas**: We embrace diverse perspectives and encourage bold hypotheses about the nature of information and reality.
* **Respectful dialogue**: Scientific debate is welcomed, but personal attacks, condescension, or hostility will not be tolerated.
* **Constructive feedback**: Suggestions should be focused on improvement and scientific rigor.
* **Credit and transparency**: Contributions will be attributed. All voices matter.
* **Reproducibility**: We value clear documentation and reproducible experiments.

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

* Discrimination, harassment, or exclusion based on identity or background
* Aggressive or demeaning language
* Plagiarism or misrepresentation of ideas
* Spam, trolling, or off-topic disruptions
* Presenting unverified claims as established facts

---

## 🧭 Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, temporary exclusion, or removal from the project, depending on severity. Issues can be reported via GitHub or directly to the project maintainer (Iban Borràs).

---

## 🤝 Let’s build a space of meaningful emergence

We are exploring how order and complexity can arise from primordial information. Let’s ensure our community reflects those same principles.

Thank you for helping create a space of respect, scientific curiosity, and shared wonder.
